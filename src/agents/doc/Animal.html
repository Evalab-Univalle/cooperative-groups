    class Animal

    Description¶ ↑

    Agent that can be one of two types: altruist (eats less than 50% of the plant's energy) or selfish (eats more than 50%)

    Energy of the animal

    identifier[R]

    Identifier on the database

    posX[R]

    posY[R]
